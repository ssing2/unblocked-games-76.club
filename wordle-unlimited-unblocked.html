How to Play

  • Step 1: Type in any five-letter word to start. Hit enter and see what happens!
  • Step 2: The game will color the letters: green means the letter is in the right spot, yellow means it’s in the word but in the wrong spot, and gray means it’s not in the word at all.
  • Step 3: Use the clues to guess the secret word in six tries or less. Think hard, but don’t overthink!
  • Step 4: If you get stuck, try common vowels or switch up your starting word. Sometimes a lucky guess is all you need!
  • Step 5: Once you solve it, you can play again instantly—no waiting for tomorrow’s puzzle!

Pro Tips

  • Start with words that use lots of vowels, like “audio” or “raise.”
