How to Play

Getting started with Vex 7 is super easy, but mastering it will take serious skill:

  • Controls: Use the WASD or arrow keys to move. W/Up to jump, A/Left to move left, D/Right to move right, and S/Down to slide.
  • Wall Jumping: When next to a wall, press the jump key to perform a wall jump and reach higher areas.
  • Checkpoints: Touch checkpoints (orange flags) to save your progress within a level.
  • Level Completion: Reach the exit portal at the end of each level to progress.
  • Deaths: Don't worry about dying - it's part of the learning process! You'll respawn at the last checkpoint.

The key to success in Vex 7 is patience and practice. You'll likely die many times before completing a level, but each attempt teaches you something new about the obstacles and timing required. Keep trying, and you'll eventually make it through even the toughest challenges!

Pro Tips & Strategies

Want to up your Vex 7 game? Check out these pro strategies that will help you conquer even the most brutal levels:

  1. Study the Level: Before making challenging jumps, take a moment to observe the pattern of moving obstacles. Many deaths can be avoided by simply waiting for the right moment.
  2. Master Wall Jumping: Wall jumps are essential for navigating many levels. Practice bouncing between two vertical walls to reach higher areas - this technique is crucial for later levels.
  3. Use Momentum: Sometimes you need to build up speed before a big jump. Run first, then jump for maximum distance.
  4. Short Hops: Not every jump needs to be at maximum height. For precise landings on small platforms, tap the jump button lightly for a controlled short hop.
  5. Sliding is Key: The slide move (down arrow/S key) is perfect for avoiding overhead obstacles and fitting through tight spaces. It can also help maintain momentum.
  6. One Obstacle at a Time: When facing a complex section, focus on overcoming one obstacle at a time rather than trying to rush through everything at once.
  7. Take Breaks: If you're stuck on a particularly challenging level, sometimes the best strategy is to take a short break. Coming back with fresh eyes often helps you see solutions you missed before.

Remember, Vex 7 is designed to be challenging - don't get discouraged by frequent deaths! Each failure brings you one step closer to mastering the level.
