Spider Solitaire

Yo, if you’re looking for a chill but brainy card game, Spider Solitaire is where it’s at! Whether you’re trying to kill time in class or just want to flex your strategy skills, this classic is always a win. No downloads, no paywalls, just pure solitaire action right here on UBG98.

How to Play

  • Step 1: The goal is to stack all the cards in descending order from King down to Ace in the same suit. Once you complete a full sequence, it disappears!
  • Step 2: You can move cards around by dragging them onto another card that’s one rank higher. For example, put a 9 on a 10, or a Jack on a Queen.
  • Step 3: Empty columns are clutch! You can move any card or a sequence of cards there to help organize your stacks.
  • Step 4: When you run out of moves, hit the deck at the bottom right to deal a new row of cards. But heads up—every column needs at least one card before you can deal!
  • Step 5: Keep going until you clear all the cards or run out of moves. Try to finish with as few moves as possible for a high score!

Game Features

  • Classic Vibes: The OG solitaire look and feel, just like on your grandma’s computer.
  • Unblocked at School: Play anytime, anywhere—no downloads, no restrictions.
  • Multiple Difficulty Levels: Choose 1, 2, or 4 suits for easy, medium, or hard mode.
  • Undo Button: Messed up? Just hit undo and try again!
  • High Score Tracker: See how you stack up against your friends.
  • Instant Restarts: Start a new game whenever you want—no waiting!

Pro Tips

  • Try to keep empty columns open—they’re super useful for moving big stacks.
  • Focus on building full sequences in the same suit for easy clears.
  • Don’t rush to deal new cards—make as many moves as you can first.
  • Use the undo button if you get stuck. No shame in a redo!
  • Practice makes perfect—the more you play, the better you get at spotting moves.
