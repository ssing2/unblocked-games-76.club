How to Play

Bob The Robber 2 has straightforward controls that are easy to learn:

  • Move: Use the arrow keys or WASD to move Bob left and right
  • Jump: Press Up Arrow, W, or Spacebar to jump
  • Crouch: Press Down Arrow or S to crouch under obstacles
  • Interact: Press X or E to interact with objects (pick locks, disable cameras, open doors)
  • Hide: Stand in shadows to become nearly invisible to guards
  • Knock Out: Approach a guard from behind and press X or E to temporarily knock them out

The goal in each level is to find and steal all the valuable items, then locate and escape through the exit door. Be careful not to be seen by guards or caught by security cameras! If you're spotted, you'll need to restart the level.
