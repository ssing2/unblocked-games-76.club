💡 Advanced Strategies for Next-Level Play

The Lean Game: Mastering when to lean forward and backward is crucial. Leaning forward gives you more speed on downhill sections and helps with landing jumps, while leaning backward can help you clear higher obstacles and control your descent.

Momentum Management: Learn to carry momentum through sections rather than constantly accelerating and braking. Sometimes coasting through a section is faster than trying to power through it.

Pattern Recognition: Each level has a rhythm and flow. Once you understand the pattern, you can anticipate what's coming and prepare accordingly. This is especially important for the more complex later levels.

Failure Analysis: Instead of getting frustrated by crashes, use them as learning opportunities. Each failure tells you something about the physics or timing that you can apply to your next attempt.
