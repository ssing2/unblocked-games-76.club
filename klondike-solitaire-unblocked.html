Foundation Strategy: Always prioritize building your foundation piles (the four stacks that go Ace through King). These are your endgame, so get those Aces out as soon as you see them!

Empty Column Power: Empty tableau columns are super valuable! You can only place Kings in empty spaces, so save them for when you really need the flexibility to rearrange your cards.
